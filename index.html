Read about the data you can track below!

  • Diet
    Doctors & patients can use this form of data tracking to monitor calories, time of meals/snacks, & more.
  • Alcohol Consumption
    Doctors can use this data to monitor how much alcohol patients consume, & to determine whether this amount is safe, or any lifestyle changes necessary.
  • Physical Data
    Doctors can use this data for common relationship diagnosis. Based on a patient's height, weight, & race a variety of information can be determined.
  • Visits
    This feature can be used both by doctors & patients in order to track visit information, and set up future appointments.
  • Vaccine History
    Track Detailed Vaccine History from this pane to ensure that your history is up to date and all necessary vaccines have been taken.
  • Prescriptions
    Monitor past prescriptions to determine whether they need to be renewed, & add prescribed medicine to this list in real-time.
